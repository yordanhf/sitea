Who Are We ?

Expert Psychologist

With our expert team, we provide services to our guests in order to provide an environment where they feel comfor and safe, not judged, and most importantly, they can be understood. We would like to meet with you in order to solve the problems, desperation, in-betweenness and burnout that people experience in their inner world.

Why Choose Us?

In our center, our psychological counseling and guidance services are based on volunteerism. It is very important for the service to reach its purpose that our experts are willing to give help and our clients to be willing to receive help. In addition to this, while serving our purpose, Unconditional Acceptance and Respect are taken as a basis. Respect for individual differences is one of our priorities.
